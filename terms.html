1. What the Service is

MyC is a private community platform for residential buildings. Residents can see building notices, post in a community feed, list items in a building marketplace, organise events, and submit maintenance requests. Building admins manage notices and maintenance through the MyC Admin app.

Everything you see and post is scoped to your own building. Residents of other buildings cannot see you or your content.

2. Who can use it

We (or your building admin) may remove accounts that misrepresent residency or identity.

This applies to feed posts, comments, marketplace listings, events, photos, and any other content you submit.

5. Moderation, reporting, and blocking

  • Automatic hiding: content reported by multiple residents is automatically hidden pending review.
  • Block: you can block any user. You will no longer see their posts, comments, listings, or events. You can unblock from your profile.
  • Enforcement: we may remove content and suspend or permanently remove accounts that violate these rules, with or without prior notice. Repeated or serious violations result in ejection from the Service.

  • You are responsible for the accuracy of your listings and for complying with UAE law regarding what may be sold.
  • Meet and exchange items safely — ideally in common areas of your building.
