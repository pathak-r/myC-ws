Account

Delete your account.

Three ways to delete your MyC account and the data we hold about you. Pick whichever is easiest.

[ 01 ]

In the app

The fastest way. Open MyC, go to your profile, and tap Delete Account.

Profile → Edit Profile → Delete Account
[ 02 ]

By email

Send us a deletion request from the email address on your account, so we can verify it's you.

hello@mycommunityuae.com
[ 03 ]

By WhatsApp

Message us with the phone number registered on your account.

+971 56 787 4381

What gets deleted

When you delete your MyC account, we remove:

  • Your name, email address, mobile number, and apartment number
  • Your profile photo (if you uploaded one)
  • Your posts in the community feed
  • Your marketplace listings (active and sold)
  • Events you created
  • Maintenance requests you submitted
  • Push notification tokens linked to your devices

What may be retained

Some information may be kept after account deletion:

  • Anonymised analytics — aggregated, non-identifying data about how the app is used. This cannot be traced back to you.
  • Information we are legally required to keep — e.g. records relevant to a dispute, regulatory request, or legal obligation under UAE law.
  • Building records held by your building admin — maintenance requests and announcements may be retained by your building manager for their own record-keeping, separate from your personal account.
  • Backups — for a short window before being overwritten.

How long it takes

In-app deletion is immediate. Email and WhatsApp requests are processed within a reasonable timeframe, typically within a few business days. You'll get a confirmation message once your account has been deleted.

Before you delete

Account deletion is permanent. We cannot recover an account once it's been deleted, even if you ask later.

If you're having trouble with the app and considering deletion as a workaround, please get in touch first — hello@mycommunityuae.com. We'd rather help you fix it.

Questions

See our privacy policy for details on data handling, or email hello@mycommunityuae.com.
